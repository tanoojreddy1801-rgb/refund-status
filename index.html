How this becomes useful:
  • The client signs in on the official Income Tax portal only.
  • A browser extension reads the filed-return page after login and extracts only PAN, AY, status, and date.
  • The extension opens this webpage and auto-fills the result summary for the client-facing response.
Why this is safer: How this works:
  • No password field exists on your webpage.
  • No credential relay or third-party login automation is used.
  • Your product still adds value through extraction, formatting, explanation, and presentation.
  • Office staff upload the latest Excel on the secure office page.
  • The workbook is parsed and its rows are published into Firebase Firestore.
  • This client page queries the latest centralized record by PAN and assessment year.
